The Stroop Test

For this experiment, you must read a list of words, but you must say the colour of the word. Not the word itself. For example, for the word, GREEN, you should say "Blue."

Once the words appear, read the list as fast as you can. When you are finished, click the "Finished!" button.

The test will be done two times. The first will be easy, while the second one will be a little more difficult.
